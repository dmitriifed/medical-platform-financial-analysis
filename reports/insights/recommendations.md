# Recommendations

## 1. Shift Demand Toward Video Consultations
- Promote video over chat
- Adjust UI prioritization
- Incentivize video bookings
- Improve video availability

## 2. Reduce Cancellations
- Implement confirmation flows
- Introduce cancellation penalties
- Improve doctor availability sync
- Add automated reminders

## 3. Optimize Service Mix
- De-emphasize low-margin chat
- Bundle chat + video
- Introduce minimum pricing
- Standardize pricing ranges

## 4. Expand High-Profit Specialties
Focus supply growth on:
- Endocrinology
- General medicine
- Surgery-related consultations
- Cardio/internal medicine

## 5. Reduce Revenue Concentration
- Recruit more active doctors
- Balance demand distribution
- Improve discovery for long-tail doctors
- Incentivize new provider onboarding

## 6. Improve Patient Retention
- Follow-up consultation prompts
- Care pathways
- Subscription packages
- Chronic care programs

## 7. Improve Unit Economics
- Optimize doctor compensation
- Introduce dynamic pricing
- Reduce low-margin consultations
- Increase average consultation value

## 8. Scale Sustainable Growth
- Focus on profitability per consultation
- Prioritize high-margin segments
- Improve operational efficiency
- Build repeat patient base
